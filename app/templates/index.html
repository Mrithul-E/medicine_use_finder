Medicine Safety Tips

  • Always Read Labels: Check the expiry date and dosage instructions carefully.
  • Avoid Self-Medication: Use this tool for information, but always follow a doctor's prescription.
  • Complete the Course: If prescribed antibiotics, complete the full course even if you feel better.
  • Storage Matters: Keep medicines in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
